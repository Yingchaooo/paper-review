# Peer review report

## Recommendation
Choose one when explicitly requested:

- Accept
- Minor revision
- Major revision
- Reject

## 1. Manuscript summary
Summarize the research question, approach, main findings, and claimed contribution. Use 3-4 sentences.

## 2. Strengths

List the strongest aspects of the work.

## 3. Major concerns

For each concern, provide:

1. Location in the manuscript
2. Specific problem
3. Why it affects validity or interpretation
4. Evidence or clarification needed
5. Whether it is potentially resolvable
6. About 30-100 words

## 4. Minor concerns

List localized issues involving clarity, presentation, terminology, figures, or reporting.
